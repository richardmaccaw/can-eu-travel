How it works:
  • Export your location history from the Google Maps app. Settings > Your timeline > Location and privacy settings > Export timeline data.
  • Drop the file into the box above or click to browse.
  • Our tool instantly analyzes your travel dates, checks which days were spent inside the Schengen Area and applies the rolling 90/180-day visa rule.
  • See your remaining Schengen days, a calendar heatmap of your visits
