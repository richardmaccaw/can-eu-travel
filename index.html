How does it work?

  • Export your location history from the Google Maps app (Settings > Your timeline > Location and privacy settings > Export timeline data).
  • Drop the exported file into the box above or click to browse.
  • We instantly analyze your travel dates, check which days were spent inside the Schengen Area, and apply the rolling 90/180-day visa rule.
  • See your remaining Schengen days and a calendar heatmap of your visits—all in your browser, with zero data leaving your device.
